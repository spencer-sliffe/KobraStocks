What role does finance play in the economy?

Finance is crucial for allocating resources efficiently, supporting economic growth, and managing risks in the economy through investment, lending, and forecasting.

What are basic financial instruments and their uses?

Basic financial instruments include stocks, bonds, and derivatives, used for investment, raising capital, hedging risks, and diversifying portfolios.

Why are credit scores important and how are they calculated?

Credit scores assess a borrower's creditworthiness based on past borrowing history, outstanding debts, and repayment timeliness, impacting loan terms and interest rates.

Where did banking originate and what was the concept of money?

Banking originated in ancient Mesopotamia with the concept of money evolving from barter systems to physical currency, facilitating trade and economic activities.

What impact does social media have on investment trends?

Social media platforms can amplify trends and mobilize individual investors, significantly impacting market movements and investor behavior.

What defines the student loan debt crisis and its impact?

The student loan debt crisis is characterized by escalating education costs, burgeoning student debt, and its stifling effect on graduates' financial freedom and economic participation.
